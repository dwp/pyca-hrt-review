Feature
Payment details
Related stories
JIRA-123

Changes

In this version, we added the option to pay into a building society account, on the payment details page.

Hypothesis

If we add the ability to pay into building society accounts, then we will see an increase in successful claims and a reduction in complaints, because users with building society accounts will be able to complete the online journey.

Research notes

Users did not see the button to switch to a building society account.

When asked what they would do next, 3 out of 4 users said they would phone up to complete their claim.

More work is needed to improve the design of this feature.
